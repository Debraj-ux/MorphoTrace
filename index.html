About LeafTrace

LeafTrace is a QR-based digital plant documentation platform developed by the students of the Department of Botany, Sripat Singh College. It serves as a comprehensive digital page showcasing detailed information about all the plants on the college campus. By scanning the QR codes attached to the trees, anyone can instantly access scientific data, photographs, medicinal uses, etc making plant identification interactive and effortless. Students can scan QR codes attached to plants and instantly access scientific information, photographs, medicinal uses, and many more. This innovative approach not only enhances the learning experience but also promotes environmental awareness and conservation efforts within the college community.
